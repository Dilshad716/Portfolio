Project Overview

Tic Tac Toe 2 RPS Mode is a strategic twist on classic Tic-Tac-Toe featuring Rock-Paper-Scissors counters and timing. In this version, both teams have a limited set of Rock, Paper, and Scissors moves.

You can capture enemy tiles by playing a move that beats theirs - Paper beats Rock, Scissors beats Paper, and so on. Outsmart your opponent with perfect timing and clever counters!

Key Features

  • Rock-Paper-Scissors mechanics
  • Strategic tile capturing
  • Limited move sets
  • Timing-based gameplay
  • Classic Tic Tac Toe rules
  • Counter-based strategy
