Project Overview

Pixel Rush is a fast-paced, minimalist tile-dash game where every move counts! Dash through a grid of pixel tiles, avoid traps, and reach your goal while particle effects and visual feedback bring the action to life.

Powered by a custom 2D array system, dynamic post-processing, and interactive effects - it's simple to pick up, but satisfying to master. Just a prototype for now... but maybe one day it'll hit the stores!

Key Features

  • Fast-paced tile-dash gameplay
  • Trap avoidance mechanics
  • Particle effects and visual feedback
  • Custom 2D array system
  • Dynamic post-processing
  • Interactive effects
  • Simple yet satisfying mastery curve
