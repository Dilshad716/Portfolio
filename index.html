About Me

I’m a dedicated Unity Developer with over two years of experience building mid-core, casual, and hyper-casual games for PC and mobile. My journey began out of curiosity and passion for interactive entertainment, and since then I’ve focused on turning creative ideas into polished, playable experiences.

I specialize in Unity, C# programming, and gameplay systems, with additional skills in networking (Netcode, Relay, Lobby systems), Firebase authentication, and Shader Graph for unique visual effects. My projects range from prototypes to full game systems, with a strong emphasis on smooth gameplay, optimization, and engaging design.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy experimenting with new mechanics, exploring art styles like monochromatic and silhouette, and learning new tools and workflows to stay ahead in game development.
